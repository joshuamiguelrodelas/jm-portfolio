Who I Am?

Background

I'm Joshua Miguel Rodelas, a third-year BS Information Technology student at the University of the Immaculate Conception, Davao City. My journey in the creative and technical space started in junior high school — what began as a simple hobby of making music mashups on a mobile phone has since grown into a multi-faceted career in IT, graphic design, video editing, and digital content creation.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To bridge creativity and technology — delivering work that is not only functional but visually compelling and meaningful. I aim to bring value to every project by combining technical precision with a strong creative instinct.

Vision: To grow as a well-rounded IT and creative professional who contributes to innovative digital solutions — whether through software development, design, or multimedia production.

Goals & Motivation

Short-term, I'm focused on sharpening my technical and design skills while gaining real-world experience through projects and freelance work. Long-term, I aim to lead creative and tech-driven initiatives — potentially in UI/UX, content production, or project management. I'm driven by the satisfaction of turning ideas into reality and the challenge of constantly learning something new.
